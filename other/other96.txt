As the world progresses, the need for innovative ways to work from anywhere in the world is on the rise. And that's where remote work steps in. IntelliJ IDEA has taken inspiration from this trend and is equipped with features to enhance your remote workflows! Remote Development Ultimate Remote Development functionality splits your IDE into two components: a local lightweight client that provides you with a responsive IntelliJ IDEA UX and the remote server that handles all of the heavy processing. You can enable this functionality from IntelliJ IDEA’s Welcome screen or via JetBrains Gateway in the JetBrains Toolbox App. Whether you’ll be working from the comfort of your home or at your local office, our next email will help you personalize the look and feel of IntelliJ IDEA to suit your personal tastes and workspace!
