Your IDE can do much more than just look good; you can personalize it to become a productivity powerhouse! Let's kick things off with some essential settings. Colored project headers You won’t be confused with multiple open projects because you can give each project a distinct color and icon, making it easier to navigate within your workspace. Plugins In Preferences | Settings / Plugins / Marketplace, you’ll find thousands of plugins for IntelliJ IDEA. Check out the Import from Postman Collections plugin, or brighten up your IDE with the pretty Nyan Progress Bar! Accessibility JetBrains is committed to making our products accessible to everyone. IntelliJ IDEA lets you enable a screen reader and adjust font size and colors. There’s also a built-in high-contrast theme that can adjust the colors for red-green color vision deficiency.
